Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is the deposit refundable?
    No, the deposit is non-refundable. It secures your spot and the balance can be paid as per the package terms.
  • Do you accommodate large groups?
    Yes, we cater to all group sizes. Contact us for group rates and special arrangements.
  • How far in advance should I book?
    We recommend booking early, especially during peak seasons, to secure your spot. We're available 24/7.
  • What happens if my trip is canceled?
    If we cancel due to unforeseen issues, you'll get a full refund or reschedule option. For personal cancellations, please check our policy.
